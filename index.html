About This Site

This site is designed to help skaters, parents, and coaches easily navigate the U.S. Figure Skating test structure. Each category breaks down the required elements, notes, and official sources so you can quickly understand what’s needed at every level. Whether you're preparing for your next test, learning the pathway through the sport, or simply curious about how the system works, this guide provides a clear, organized reference to support your skating journey.
